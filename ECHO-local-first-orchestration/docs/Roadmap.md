- Philosophy and doctrine framing
- Documentation suite
- Local-first rationale

## Phase 2 — Refinement (In Progress)
- Expanded design principles
- Improved diagrams
- Public examples (non-sensitive)
- Community discussion

## Phase 3 — Tooling
- Optional n8n workflow templates
- Local-first starter kits
- Developer onboarding materials

## Phase 4 — Evolution
- Meta-alignment processes
- Long-term refinement cycles
- Extended cognitive scaffolding

ECHO evolves slowly and intentionally.
